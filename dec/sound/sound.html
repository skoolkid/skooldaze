Theme tune
This tune is played by the routines at 32390 (just after the game has loaded) and 32400 (before a new game starts and when ERIC has gone up a year).
All shields/opened safe tune
This tune is played by the routine at 32406 when the 15th shield has started flashing and when the safe has been opened.
Bell
The bell sound effect is produced by the routine at 26450.
Lines
These sound effects are produced by the routine at 30464 when a teacher is giving lines to ERIC, EINSTEIN, ANGELFACE or BOY WANDER. The second sound effect is also used when a teacher reveals his safe combination letter.
Catapult
This sound effect is produced by the routine at 65141 when ERIC fires the catapult.
Shield
This sound effect is produced by the routine at 58604 when a shield has been hit.
Knocked out
This sound effect is produced by the routine at 65111 when ERIC has been knocked over or pushed out of a seat.
Hitting
This sound effect is produced by the routine at 60128 when ERIC throws a punch.
Jumping
This sound effect is produced by the routine at 60139 when ERIC jumps.
Walking
These sound effects are produced by the routine at 65088 each time ERIC takes a step.
