79BA: Control EINSTEIN during class (1)
Used by command lists 144, 152, 160 and 168. Makes EINSTEIN wait for his turn to speak, and then hands over control to the interruptible subcommand routine at 798C; when EINSTEIN has finished speaking, control returns to the primary command routine at 79CA.
