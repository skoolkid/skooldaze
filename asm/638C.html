638C: Guide a character up a staircase
The address of this interruptible subcommand routine is placed into bytes 0x69 and 0x6A of a character's buffer by the routine at 63BE when the character has reached the bottom of a staircase he needs to ascend to reach his destination.
Input
Output
638C LD B,$FF Signal that the character is going up a staircase
